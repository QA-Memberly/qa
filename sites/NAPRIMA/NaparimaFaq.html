1. What are all the features present in Naparima app?
    The following features are present in the Naparima app.
    • Profile
    • Student List
    • Recent Activity
    • Group
    • Mark Attendance
    • Attendance Register
    • Send Message
    • Portal
    • FAQ
    • Support

  2. What can be done if the user forgets the username?
    Click the “Forgot Username” hyperlink from the sign-in screen, provide the registered email address. The username will be sent to the registered email id.

  3. What can be done if the user forgets the password?
    Click the “Forgot Password” hyperlink from the sign-in screen, provide the registered email address. The password will be sent to the registered email id.

  4. How to raise queries? And How will the support work?
    Support can be reached from the login screen or from the home page. The queries which are raised by the user will be sent to the support team’s email. The team will revert within 24 hours and the response will be sent to the user’s mail id.

  5. How do I change my password?
    The password can be changed by clicking on “Settings”  Reset Password. The old password should be entered for the confirmation. The new password has to be entered twice and submitted.

  6. What can be viewed on the Student list?
    The Student list displays the list of students who are all present in that particular class.

  7. What can be viewed as recent activity?
    The activities of the Teachers i.e the message notifications. will be displayed along with the status.

  8. Whose advertisements will be displayed?
    The advertisements of the sponsors will be displayed in the Naparima app. If clicked on the advertisement, it will navigate to the corresponding website.

  9. Can Naparima promote my company by adding advertisements?
    Yes, it does. Please contact the support team.

  10. How does Group work?
    The Group can be reached from the home screen. The Set of Students form a Group.
